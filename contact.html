jemdoc – contact details

Development

jemdoc is developed by Jacob Mattingley. Please contact Jacob with any bug reports and comments. If reporting a bug, please run the command jemdoc --version and include the result in your email.

There are two levels of support:

  • Basic jemdoc, which I will attempt to support well, and

  • LaTeX equations in jemdoc, which I may attempt to support.
